What can acupuncture and massage do?

Got headaches?

Digestive trouble?

Insomnia?

Painful periods?

Cold or flu?

Chronic pain?

Anxious and depressed?

Acupuncture and massage can relieve the symptoms of these and most conditions with few side-effects!

What is acupuncture like?

Your treatment will be a comfortable and enjoyable experience.

I'll ask you questions about your health and do a lot of listening.

Your acupuncture treatment will also include cupping, oil massage or shiatsu massage at no extra cost.

Much of the time, you won't feel the insertion of the hair-thin needles.

Your treatment will be private, relaxing and will relieve stress.

What does it cost?

Treatments last about 60 minutes. The cost is $80 for acupuncture or $100 for shiatsu massage.

The Stop-Smoking Assist Program costs $400 for one month, with 2 half-hour visits per week required.

Health insurance is accepted.
