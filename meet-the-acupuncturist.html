Lanai Lewis, Licensed Acupuncturist and Chinese Herbalist

I grew up in a tiny suburb of Seattle called Bothell. It was a nice, safe place to spend a childhood.

I moved to Portland, Oregon in 2002, and fell in love with its small yet vibrant community, golden summers, phenomenal restaurants and young culture.

I pursued a degree in graphic design, graduating from Portland State University in 2008 before deciding to change my focus and study Chinese medicine at Oregon College of Oriental Medicine. My studies emphasized Chinese herbal medicine and shiatsu, in addition to my training in acupuncture. I also hold a certificate in Reiki energy healing.

When I'm not working, I salsa dance, play mandolin, practice qigong, hike and camp, cook, study French and Spanish, and ride my motorcycle.
