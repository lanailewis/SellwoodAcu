Acupuncture

Chinese medicine has long held that the human body is a whole system, each part is connected to every other. Acupuncture maps out these connections with meridians that wind their way through this system. With gentle insertion of small, hair-thin needles into "acupoints" that lie on these meridians, we can affect not only superficial areas of the body, but also deep ones. I seek to encourage the body's own natural mechanisms to balance itself and promote healing.

Your acupuncture treatment will be tailored to fit your individual needs and preferences. I will also finish your treatment with cupping, oil massage or shiatsu at no extra cost.

$80 per each acupuncture treatment without insurance.

Copays and other costs vary for acupuncture with insurance.

Shiatsu

Shiatsu massage is available separately at $100 per each treatment.

Shiatsu or "finger pressure" massage, is a Japanese style of massage that uses the same meridians as Chinese acupuncture. Shiatsu releases tension, extinguishes pain and improves flexibility.

In contrast to other types of massage, clothes remain on during the treatment.

$100 per each shiatus treatment, cash only.

Reiki and Others

Reiki is a gentle Japanese healing technique. Hands are placed on the body or near the body to gently harmonize a person's energy. I often use this technique in conjunction with acupuncture and shiatsu.

Cupping is a Chinese method that involves placing glass or plastic cups on the body. A suction is created under the cups to help release muscle tension and adhesions.

Moxibustion is a Chinese technique using the herb, mugwort. This herb is burned near the body for a variety of benefits including relief from pain and stiffness in joints, and to encourage healthy digestion.

Chinese Herbal Medicine is a powerful tool in treating illness, both externally and internally.

Meditation can help relieve anxiety, depression and physical illness. The state of the mind is very important to the state of the body. I practice a Chinese form of meditation called Qigong, and can teach you some simple but effective exercises.
